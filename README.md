# 🐘 Kerala Pooram Management Portal

## Overview

Kerala Pooram Management Portal is a centralized digital platform designed to simplify the planning and organization of temple festivals (Poorams/Ulsavams) across Kerala.

The portal connects **Temple Festival Committees**, **Elephant Owners**, and **Pooram Accessory Owners** through a single platform where festival resources can be discovered, requested, and managed efficiently.

Anyone can browse festivals, elephants, and accessory availability without creating an account, while authenticated users can manage bookings and rental requests through dedicated dashboards.

The goal is to bring transparency, organization, and convenience to Kerala's traditional temple festival ecosystem.

---

# Objectives

* Digitize the traditional Pooram planning process.
* Provide a centralized directory of Kerala temple festivals.
* Make elephant availability transparent.
* Reduce booking conflicts.
* Maintain verified elephant information.
* Simplify the rental of traditional Pooram accessories.
* Create a one-stop platform for festival committees.

# User Roles

## Public Users

No login required.

Users can:

* Browse upcoming Poorams and Ulsavams.
* Search festivals by district, temple, or date.
* Browse registered elephants.
* View elephant profiles.
* View elephant booking calendars.
* Browse Pooram accessories available for rent.
* View accessory availability calendars.
* Search elephants and accessories.
* Explore festival details.

---

## Festival Committee

Committee members can:

* Register and manage committee accounts.
* Register temples.
* Add festival information.
* Search available elephants.
* Search available Pooram accessories.
* Send booking requests.
* Track request status.
* View booking history.
* Manage committee profile.

---

## Elephant Owners

Elephant owners can:

* Register multiple elephants.
* Maintain elephant profiles.
* Upload images and documents.
* Update fitness certificate validity.
* Manage booking requests.
* Accept or reject requests.
* Confirm bookings after offline communication.
* Automatically update elephant availability calendars.

# Booking Workflow

### Elephant Booking

1. Committee searches available elephants.
2. Committee sends a booking request.
3. Elephant owner receives the request.
4. Both parties communicate externally.
5. Owner confirms the booking.
6. Calendar updates automatically.
7. Public users can instantly view the booked dates.

### Accessory Rental

1. Committee searches available accessories.
2. Committee submits a rental request.
3. Accessory owner receives the request.
4. Both parties communicate externally.
5. Owner confirms the rental.
6. Availability calendar updates automatically.
7. Booked dates become unavailable.
